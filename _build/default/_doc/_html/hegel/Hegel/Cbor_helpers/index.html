Module Hegel.Cbor_helpers

CBOR encoding/decoding with type-safe extractor helpers.

CBOR encoding/decoding helpers for the Hegel SDK.

This module provides convenience functions for encoding and decoding CBOR values, plus type-safe extractor functions that produce clear error messages when a value has an unexpected type.

type t = CBOR.Simple.t

The type of CBOR values, re-exported from CBOR.Simple.

val encode : CBOR.Simple.t -> string

encode v serializes the CBOR value v to a binary string.

val decode : string -> CBOR.Simple.t

decode s deserializes a CBOR value from the binary string s.

val type_name : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined ] -> string

type_name v returns a human-readable name for the CBOR type of v.

val extract_int : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Int ] -> 'e

extract_int v extracts an integer from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not an `Int.

val extract_float : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Float ] -> 'd

extract_float v extracts a float from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not a `Float.

val extract_string : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Text ] -> 'i

extract_string v extracts a text string from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not a `Text.

val extract_bool : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Bool ] -> 'b

extract_bool v extracts a boolean from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not a `Bool.

val extract_bytes : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Bytes ] -> 'c

extract_bytes v extracts a byte string from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not a `Bytes.

val extract_list : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Array ] -> 'a

extract_list v extracts a list of CBOR values from a CBOR value.

Raises Failure if v is not an `Array.

val extract_dict : [< `Array of 'a | `Bool of 'b | `Bytes of 'c | `Float of 'd | `Int of 'e | `Map of 'f | `Null | `Simple of 'g | `Tag of 'h | `Text of 'i | `Undefined Map ] -> 'f

extract_dict v extracts a key-value map from a CBOR value. Keys and values remain as CBOR values.

Raises Failure if v is not a `Map.

val is_null : [> `Null ] -> bool

is_null v returns true if v is `Null.
